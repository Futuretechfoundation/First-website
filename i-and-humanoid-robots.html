BCI and Humanoid Robots Track

Delve into the interface between human cognition and advanced robotics.

Courses Offered

  • Brain-Computer Interfaces (BCI): Learn how to create direct communication between brains and computers.
  • Telepathy-Based Communication: Study futuristic communication methods beyond physical interaction.
  • Neural Networks: Explore the functioning and development of neural networks for AI and robotics.
  • Mind Uploading: Investigate technologies that allow human consciousness to inhabit humanoid robots.
  • Humanoid Development: Study the construction of humanoids that mimic human behavior, emotions, and thought processes.
