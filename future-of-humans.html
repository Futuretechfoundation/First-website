Future of Humans Track

Explore the advancements in technologies aimed at enhancing human capabilities and health.

Courses Offered

  • Biotechnology: Learn about genetic engineering, synthetic biology, and bioinformatics.
  • Regenerative Biotherapeutics: Explore therapies such as stem cell therapy and tissue engineering.
  • 3D Bioprinting: Dive into 3D printing of biological structures, tissues, and organs.
  • Prosthetics: Study the design and development of artificial limbs using advanced materials.
  • Nanorobotics: Learn about nanoscale robots for cellular repair and targeted drug delivery.
  • Surgical Robotics: Explore robot-assisted surgeries and minimally invasive techniques.
